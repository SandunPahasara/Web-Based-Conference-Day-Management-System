Session Details: Advances in Communication Systems

Session Overview

Time: 10:15 AM - 11:15 AM

Speaker: Prof. Amin Abbosh

Venue: Hall A

Session Abstract: In this session, Prof. Amin Abbosh will explore the latest advancements in communication systems, with a focus on the future of 5G technology, antenna designs, and signal processing techniques. The talk will include real-world applications and challenges that engineers face in these rapidly evolving fields.

Key Topics Covered:

  • 5G and Beyond: Challenges and Opportunities
  • Wireless Communication Systems and Signal Processing
  • Antenna Design and Optimization
  • Future Trends in Communication Technology

Learning Outcomes:

  • Gain an understanding of the latest trends in communication technologies
  • Learn how advancements in antenna design are shaping wireless networks
  • Understand the impact of 5G on industries and society

About the Speaker

Prof. Amin Abbosh is a leading expert in advanced communication systems, specializing in antenna design, wireless networks, and signal processing. With over two decades of experience, he has made significant contributions to the development of next-generation communication technologies and has authored numerous research papers in top-tier journals.
